# Ulta Salon, Cosmetics & Fragrance

Ulta Salon, Cosmetics & Fragrance (now operating as Ulta Beauty) is the largest beauty retailer in the United States, offering cosmetics, fragrance, skin care products, hair care products, and salon services through retail stores and online. This entry tracks the legacy brand identity prior to the Ulta Beauty rebranding.
